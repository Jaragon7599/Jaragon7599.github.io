Carli Lloyd

  • Carli Lloyd is a two-time Olympic gold medalist and a two-time FIFA Women's World Cup champion.
  • Carli Lloyd holds the record for the fastest hat-trick in Women’s World Cup history.

Alex Morgan

  • Morgan Is One Of The Only Two Players To Score 20 Goals In One Season
  • Alex Morgan is an advocate for gender equality and was part of a wage-discrimination action against the U.S. Soccer Federation in 2016.

Abby Wombach

  • Wambach Is The American Women’s National Team’s All-Time Highest Scorer
  • Abby Wambach was awarded the 2012 FIFA World Player of the Year, becoming the first American woman to win the award in 10 years.
